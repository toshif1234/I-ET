About Us

At Stellium, we address long-standing supply chain challenges with innovative solutions. With over a decade of experience in defining and designing supply chain processes and systems, we help organizations anticipate variability, build agility, and ensure operational reactivity.

StockSnap, a cross-platform app, provides real-time data synchronization across mobile, web, and cloud systems. It's tailored to streamline stock audits, reduce errors, and offer powerful analytics to enable quicker, data-driven decisions.

Why StockSnap?

StockSnap transforms the stock-taking process by:

  • Improving stock count accuracy.
  • Reducing manual errors and data entry issues.
  • Optimizing inventory control with real-time insights.
